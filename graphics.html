Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaluated: "Apple"

Purpose of graphics on the site: Apple

the graphics on the Apple page is clean and simple. There is a contrast between the navigation bar and the images. It has a simplicity look on text and images. Clean and simple allows to focus on the content.

Gui delines for the effective use of graphics

  1. Yes - Graphics should fit in with the purpose, organization, and style of the page.
  2. Yes - Large graphics are avoided
  3. Yes - Graphics help to guide the viewers' focus to the important content on the page.
  4. Yes - Overly bright and potentially "obnoxius" images have been avoided.
  5. Yes - Graphics have not been used for textual content, other than the banner.
  6. Yes - Alternate text is present for all images.
  7. Yes - Where text appears in graphics (the banner) there is ample contrast.
